# Child Safety Policy

### Purpose
This document describes the measures taken to help protect minors and ensure safe use of the application.

### Age
The application is not specifically directed at children. Users under 18 years of age are required to follow the same rules of use as all other users.

### User-Generated Content
The application allows user-generated audio content and interactions between users.

### Safety Measures
- No public sharing of personal contact information is encouraged.
- Content that violates the rules or applicable laws may be removed.
- Accounts involved in abuse or harmful behavior may be restricted or suspended.

### Reporting and Blocking
Users can report inappropriate behavior or content directly within the application.
Users can also block other users to prevent further interaction.

### Data Handling
User data is processed only as required for core application functionality.
No personal data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising purposes.
